Who Am I?

Bio

Age: 23
Origin: Houston, Texas

Statement: I believe that great things comes from those that are meticulous. Those that obsess about the details make something, whether it be software or physical, that is perfect to most that behold the object. When one builds a moonshot product, perfection determines the success rate as most will increasingly discount the product for every known flaw. Iterations of current ideas are very boring to me, and ideas that solve abstract problems are attention keepers. Lastly, great art is only possible if the pieces that comprise the whole are also artful in nature.

"Be a yardstick of quality. Some people aren't used to an environment where excellence is expected." - Steve Jobs
