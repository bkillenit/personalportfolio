Projects

Notable projects that I have spearheaded or participated in are listed below along with a short synopsis about their functionality. Please click on the URL provided for each project to see more about its progress.

Scheduling AI (Fall 2014)
Meeting scheduling service that communicates via email and automates the process of contacting potential attendees and finding out open times of a group. Django, Regex, Stanford NLP

Huddle (Fall 2013)
Portable device that allows users to share documents and collaborate offline, intended as a modular personal cloud for rooms. Rasbian, Ruby on Rails

Smart Humidor (Summer 2014)
Embedded system that maintains user defined humidity level in humidor and communicates temperature and humidity levels to a web app. Arduino, Ruby on Rails

Splowse (Spring 2013)
Web interface that enables website viewing and bookmarking alongside posts from favorite social media platforms. Front-end languages, Ruby on Rails and Twitter API
